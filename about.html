About the Class

Located in Baranagar, our institute is dedicated to serious performers who want to master the craft of acting.

The curriculum covers a wide range of essential skills:

  • Screen Acting & Micro-expressions
  • Voice Modulation & Diction
  • Physical Theatre & Body Language
  • Psychological Character Development

Production Tie-ups

Students completing the course may receive exclusive opportunities to work in BDK Production films and drama projects.
